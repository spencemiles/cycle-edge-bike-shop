About Cycle Edge

Your Journey Starts Here

At Cycle Edge, we believe everyone has the potential to become an exceptional cyclist. Founded in 2015, we've helped thousands of riders discover their passion for cycling and push their limits.

Our state-of-the-art facility combines cutting-edge technology with expert coaching to help you achieve your cycling goals, whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced racer.

We're more than just a bike shop - we're a community of cycling enthusiasts dedicated to helping you find your edge.

Why Choose Cycle Edge

Expert Coaching

Our certified coaches have trained Olympic athletes and beginners alike with personalized training plans.

Cutting-Edge Tech

Access to the latest in bike fitting technology, performance tracking, and recovery tools.

Vibrant Community

Join group rides, workshops, and social events with fellow cycling enthusiasts.

Holistic Approach

We focus on nutrition, recovery, and mental training alongside your cycling development.
